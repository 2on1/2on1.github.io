MATTHEW MCCREARY

Writer. Editor. Designer.

When I first moved to New York City after graduating from Williams College in 2014, I knew I wanted to become a novelist and editor. Since then, I've discovered a passion and proclivity not just for the writing itself, but for the diverse techincal skills needed in the modern, digital-heavy media workspace - whether that's the Adobe Suite, social media platforms, or even coding languages, I know just how important it is to be multifaceted in this environment. That's why I made everything you see on this page, from the graphics to the layout and, of course, the words.

When I'm not working, I spend most of my time reading fantasy epics like Patrick Rothfuss' The Name of the Wind, obsessively looking at obscure NBA statistics, or playing pickup hoops.
