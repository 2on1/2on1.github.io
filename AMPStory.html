Tale of 2 Towns

What is going on with Karl-Anthony Towns in the playoffs?

Karl-Anthony Towns is one of the most talented scorers in the NBA. Through three seasons, he's averaging 21.6 points per game on 54.3% from the field, 38.7% from three, and 83.6% from the free-throw line.

Yet, in his first playoff game, Towns struggled. He took just 9 shots against the Houston Rockets, and spent much of the game standing in the corner, watching guards like Derrick Rose and Jeff Teague drive against Houston center Clint Capela.
